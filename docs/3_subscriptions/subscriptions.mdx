Suberra handles three kinds of subscription payments
<Tabs>
  <TabItem value="fixed" label="Fixed Fee" default>
    Access to all kinds of features for a single monthly or annual price. Suitable for most research platforms, entertainment tools, and software services for individuals.
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem value="usage-based" label="Usage-Based">
    Charges based on the customer's usage (e.g. $5 per GB storage used). Suitable for cloud instrastructure and computing services.
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem value="per-seat" label="Per Seat">
    Charges based on number of seats (e.g. $8 per user). Suitable for enterprise productivity products.
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>
